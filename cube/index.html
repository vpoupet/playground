Instructions

  • Hover over a sticker with the mouse and use the arrow keys or Z, Q, S, D to rotate the corresponding slice in the desired direction
  • Hold the Shift key and a direction to rotate the whole cube
  • Swipe on a sticker with one finger to rotate a slice
  • Swipe on a face with two fingers to rotate the whole cube
  • Click the "Shuffle" button to shuffle the cube (1000 random moves, not animated)
